Where To?

This was the final project at my time at Bitmaker Labs Toronto (12 week web developement boot-camp). Uploaded to Heroku and made with Ruby on Rails, this app's main idea was to pick a destination to travel to based on the popularity of the top 5 countries to visit in each month. This idea was chosen so that we could call Google Maps API and populate our data through that. We seeded our data (months, countries) one time with a schema, and called Google Maps for the hotels for more detialed information.
